About

SHULEguru is a community organization providing better environment for education services to the underprivileged and at risk children in the society in order to solve global challenges of inequity, bias and ignorance for benefits of the whole society with common humanity for generations to come. Thus; we at SHULEguru provide a supplement for the formal education system to all who do not fully enjoy it through personalized learning in order to achieve a world class quality education, knowledge and information for everyone in the society. Remember: Poor uneducated children grow up to be poor uneducated adults!
